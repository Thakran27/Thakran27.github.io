About

Curiosity into meaningful discoveries.

A researcher’s journey is driven by curiosity, where each challenge opens the door to new ideas and meaningful discoveries. I believe true innovation grows through collaboration, shared knowledge, and diverse perspectives, and I am passionate about building connections that lead to impactful scientific progress.

Research Interest

My research focuses on semiconductor, photovoltaic, and optoelectronic technologies for high-performance and sustainable energy applications. I work on materials design, interface engineering, thin-film processing, and device optimization, with emphasis on improving efficiency, stability, and scalability. My broader goal is to connect solid-state physics with practical device engineering for next-generation energy and electronic technologies.

Research & Projects

From materials to device performance.

We develop semiconductor and optoelectronic devices through an integrated workflow that includes processing, microstructure optimization, device fabrication, and performance evaluation. My strength lies in connecting material design with practical device engineering and measurable performance improvement.

Tandem Photovoltaics & Interface Engineering

I am developing monolithic two-terminal perovskite/silicon tandem solar cells, emphasizing interfacial engineering and performance analysis to improve charge transport, reduce losses, and enable efficient, scalable tandem photovoltaic devices.

Advanced Packaging Workshop

Completed technical training in semiconductor packaging fundamentals, design, and testing, including ANSYS-based exercises with documented reports and simulation files.

Experimental Skills

Fabrication, characterization, and analysis.

An integrated experimental toolkit spanning thin-film device fabrication, advanced spectroscopy and microscopy, and quantitative device analysis.

Design & Fabrication

High-performance perovskite solar cells with optimized interfaces, photodetectors, ball-milling, PDMS transfer printing, spin-coating, and spray pyrolysis.

Advanced Characterization

Transient absorption spectroscopy, photoluminescence, optical microscopy, CV, Ramé-Hart goniometry, laser alignment, ALD, PE-CVD, Raman, SEM, AFM, XRD, and XPS.

Data Analysis

Device performance evaluation, materials–property correlation, interface and failure analysis, and interpretation of characterization results for process optimization.
